About Us The 1975


Biography of the band "The 1975":

"The 1975" is a British pop rock band formed in Manchester in 2002. The band consists of four main members: Matthew Healy (vocals and guitar), Adam Hann (guitar), Ross MacDonald (bass), and George Daniel (drums). The current lineup was solidified in 2012. The band took its name from a date marked in an old book Healy found in the home of a former schoolmate. They began to gain recognition with their independent music and landed a record deal in 2012. Their eponymous debut album, "The 1975," was released in 2013, receiving critical acclaim and establishing them as a force in the music scene. "The 1975" is known for their eclectic sound that fuses elements of pop, rock, synth-pop and R&B. They have released several successful albums, such as "I Like It When You Sleep, for You Are So Beautiful yet So Unaware of It" (2016) and "A Brief Inquiry into Online Relationships" (2018). In addition to music, the band is also recognized for their visual aesthetic and thoughtful lyrics.

Biography of Matthew Healy:

Matthew Timothy Healy was born on April 8, 1989, in London, England. He is the son of British actors Tim Healy and Denise Welch. From a young age, Matthew showed a passionate interest in music and art. He grew up in a family with a strong presence in the entertainment industry, which exposed him early on to creativity and artistic expression. At the age of 13, Matthew moved to Manchester, where he attended Manchester Grammar School. It was at the school where he met his future band mates and began to explore his talent for music. Inspired by a variety of musical genres, Matthew developed an eclectic approach that would later become a hallmark of "The 1975". His unique voice and ability to write profound lyrics have become key elements of the band's success. Throughout his career, Matthew Healy has publicly faced personal struggles, including his battle with addiction. However, he has used his experience to advocate for mental health and social awareness through the music of The 1975. His charismatic energy on stage and commitment to authenticity have won the admiration of fans and critics alike.

Biography of Adam Hann:

Adam Brian Thomas Hann was born on June 20, 1988, in Manchester, England. His connection to music began early, as his father worked as a musician and introduced him to the world of guitar. Adam quickly developed impressive skills on the instrument and became an essential component of The 1975's distinctive sound. After attending Manchester Grammar School, Adam met Matthew Healy, Ross MacDonald and George Daniel, with whom he formed the band. His experimental approach and ability to create unique guitar riffs have been crucial elements in the musical evolution of "The 1975". In addition to his role as lead guitarist, Adam has been a driving force behind the band's distinctive visual aesthetic.

Biography of Ross MacDonald:

Ross Stewart MacDonald was born on July 1, 1989, in Manchester, England. From an early age, he showed an interest in music and embarked on an exploration of the electric bass. His technical ability and solid understanding of music theory quickly highlighted him as a talented musician. Ross joined Matthew Healy, Adam Hann and George Daniel to form "The 1975". His contribution as bassist has been instrumental in creating the musical foundation that defines the band's sonic identity. His ability to fuse different genres and styles has been key to the success and versatility of The 1975's music.

Biography of George Daniel:

George Bedford Daniel was born on May 26, 1990, in Manchester, England. His love for music and the drums led him to become a talented percussionist at a young age. George not only demonstrated exceptional drumming skills, but also an innate talent for music production. As drummer and producer of The 1975, George has played an essential role in the evolution of the band's sound. His creativity and experimental approach have been crucial elements in the band's ability to explore new musical territory. In addition to his work with The 1975, George has collaborated with other artists and has gained recognition for his contribution to the world of contemporary music production.
